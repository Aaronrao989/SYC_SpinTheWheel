ABOUT THE EVENT

The Student Welfare Department, in association with Sanjeevani & Yoga Club, is back with WALKATHON 2.0 – The 10-Day Elite Grind!

After the massive success of last year's Walkathon, where 484 participants actively stepped towards a healthier lifestyle, we are excited to bring it back — bigger, better, and without limits!

Last year, due to certain foundational constraints, registrations were capped. But this time — no limits, just competition and consistency!

Proudly initiated by S.Y.C., this is a one-of-its-kind fitness event in the NCR region. 💪

🎮 Theme: Pokémon GO — Walk your way to becoming a Pokémon Master! Every step counts in this real-world adventure.
